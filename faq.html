How long do items typically take to arrive after purchase?

All products should arrive after 2-3 business days for standard delivery, however premium delivery will guarantee they are delivered after 1 business day.

Can I send my item back if I change my mind?

You can contact us and send back the item within 30 days and receive a full refund.

When does warranty expire?

We have a warranty agreement that states all products are covered for 12 months. You can verify this at the bottom of the page.

Are all of the goods tracked with ID?

After ordering an item, you will be sent an email with a tracking ID assigned to it to check the status.

What if my item is faulty after arriving?

If a product is not in working order, report it by contacting us for collection and replacement.
